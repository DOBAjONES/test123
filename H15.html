LONDON

LONDON is the capital of England. it is the most populous city in the United kingdom with a metropolitan area of over 13 million inhabitants

Standing on River Thames, London has been a major settlement for two millennia, its history is going back to its founding by the Roman, who name it Londinium.

PARIS

Paris is the capital and most populous city of France.

TOKYO

Tokyo is the capital of Japan, the center of the Greater Tokyo Area, and the most populous metropolitan area in the world.
